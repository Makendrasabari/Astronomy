About Stackly.

✦

We are passionate about astronomy and dedicated to bringing the universe closer to you. Our mission is to inspire curiosity, promote learning, and explore the cosmos together.

Learn More

Our Vision

A world inspired by the wonders of the universe, where astronomical knowledge is accessible, empowering every stargazer to connect with the infinite cosmos.

Our Mission

To educate, engage and explore the cosmos by connecting communities with state-of-the-art telemetry, telescopes, and real-time celestial coordinates.

Our Values

Curiosity, discovery, and scientific integrity. We promote dark sky preservation and foster global educational collaborations across classrooms.

Our Journey

How Stackly Was Born

Stackly was founded in late 2021 by a collective of stargazers, telescope designers, and software engineers who believed stargazing should not be restricted by expensive gear or city smog.

By integrating web dashboards directly with high-altitude lenses in remote deserts, we created an active ecosystem where users can request telescope times and download coordinates easily.
